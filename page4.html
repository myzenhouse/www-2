The platforms used by a digital marketing agency

  • Facebook
  • Google Analytics
  • Mailchimp
  • HubSpot

Facebook is a social media giant with a vast user base. Digital marketing agencies leverage Facebook for advertising, content distribution, and audience engagement through pages and groups.

A powerful analytics tool, Google Analytics provides insights into website traffic, user behavior, and conversion data. Digital marketers use it to assess campaign performance and optimize strategies.

Mailchimp is an email marketing platform that enables agencies to create, send, and analyze email campaigns. It offers features like automation, segmentation, and A/B testing for effective communication.

HubSpot is an all-in-one platform for inbound marketing, sales, and customer service. Digital marketing agencies use HubSpot for content creation, lead generation, CRM, and marketing automation.
